Australian Farming In Focus
This data visualization offers a comprehensive look into the Australian farming sector, highlighting its significance as the second largest industry in the country. By examining crop yields, livestock statistics, and water usage, it becomes evident how Australia's agricultural landscape is shaped by climatic conditions and global competitiveness.
Yield in Hectares For Summer and Winter Crops - 2023
Australian winter crops yield significantly more than summer crops due to the country's high summer temperatures and the soil's lower moisture retention.
